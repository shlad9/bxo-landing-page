Support and Contact

Last Updated: April 2026

Contact Email

For marketing inquiries, support, collaborations, partnerships, press, or anything else, email: unscene.app@gmail.com

What We Can Help With

  • Account and login issues
  • Bug reports and troubleshooting
  • Feature requests and product feedback
  • Marketing, press, and media inquiries
  • Collaborations and partnerships

Tips for Faster Help

  • Include your device model and OS version (iOS/Android)
  • Share screenshots or screen recordings when relevant
  • Describe the steps to reproduce the issue
  • If it’s a collaboration, include links and a brief proposal

Response Time

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

Privacy Note

Please don’t email sensitive personal information (like passwords). If you’re reporting an account issue, share only what’s needed for us to help.

You can also review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
