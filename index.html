About Us

Nice to meet you! We are Mahek (CEO, BxO) and Swapnil (CTO, BxO) - students at Harvard Business School building the future of content.

What started as a long conversation about anime TV shows and Bollywood movies in New Zealand, turned into an idea to create a place for people to share their thoughts on content with their friends in a fun, easy, and community-based manner.
